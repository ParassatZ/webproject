WHO WE ARE

New Old Boys. It all began in Paris in 1966. We started Perfumery with two founders, a passion for genuine perfumes, and a vision to change the game. They set out with a dream to disrupt the outdated perfumery industry and make quality perfumery more accessible.

From our early days shooting our own photos and packaging every order ourselves, to a fast growing company with a team of more than 50, our values have remained the same. Now based in Pennsylvania, USA and shipping around the globe, we’re still committed to stunning, hand selected perfumery and personal service.

Always authentic cologne

Aroma, beautiful colognes are at the heart of our brand. We work exclusively with authentic, natural items, and many customers appreciate the metaphysical properties and everyday magic they bring to their lives. We pay close attention to detail during the design process, finding balance between modern aesthetics and timeless lines. Our aromas are – and will always be – 100% conflict free.
